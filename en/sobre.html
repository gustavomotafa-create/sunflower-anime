About Sunflower Anime

Sunflower Anime is a space created for anime fans who enjoy following theories, curiosities, memorable characters and organized content all in one place.

Our goal is to share information in a simple, pleasant way, with care for people who truly love this otaku universe.

Here you can find content about popular anime, well-known characters, comparisons, curious facts and news from the anime world.

The site was developed to offer easy navigation, a pleasant visual experience and content designed for people who like exploring this universe in a practical way.
